Welcome to Decentralized Resource using Hyperledger Composer


This Blockchain application demonstrates resource sharing on a network among 'Owners' on a grid. The resource trade is based on exchanging coins for resource provided. The network also includes 'Companies' to trade excess resource with or obtain resource from, and 'Banks' to exchange cash (fiat money) for coins.

Find more detail about the application.


Step1 - Create and Update Participants

Owners own three assets: Resource, Coins and Cash. They can trade with fellow Owner, banks, and companies.

Owner

Banks on the network own two assets: Cash and Coins. They can trade with Owner, who can buy or sell coins for cash.

Banks

Companies own two assets: Resource and Coins. They can trade with Owner, who can buy or sell resource for coins.
